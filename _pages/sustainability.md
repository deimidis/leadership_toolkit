## Sustainability: Mozillians Support, Build and Create New Knowledge

By programming and fixing a bug, facilitating a hackathon, contributing to open source content and developing resources that describe how to do any of things, Mozillians are building and creating knowledge for others. They have a deep understanding of technology and they are able to connect their technological abilities with community engagement. The connection between human interactions and technology increases their social capital. Social

> "**Social capital** is a form of [economic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capital_(economics)) and [cultural capital](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cultural_capital) in which [social networks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_network) are central, [transactions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stock_and_flow) are marked by [reciprocity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reciprocity_social_psychology), [trust](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trust_(social_sciences)), and [cooperation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cooperation), and [market](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Market_(economics)) [agents](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agent_(economics)) produce [goods and services](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Goods_and_services) not mainly for themselves, but for a [common good](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Common_good)."  [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_capital)

### Activities that Build and Create New Knowledge:

* **Mozilla on Campus**
  * Demonstrate strategic thinking
  * Demonstrate creative problem solving


* **Copyright/MakerParty**
   * Come up with new ways to demonstrate the importance of the issue

* **Bring people into open source**
   * Learn and be able to explain why open source matters

* **Updated events + tools**
   * Deliver high quality event: excellence from logistics to content and follow up
    * Control logistics for events
    * Measure after-event outcomes

* **Community Spaces**
   * Schedule and get different types of events happening

* **Community Experimentation Launchpad**
   * Develop creative ideas
   * Think out of the box curation,
   * Lead and develop an initial idea into a more mature/sustainable project
